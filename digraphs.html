PHRASES USING DIGRAPH - DJ

DJA Bu ta trabadja manhan?
​ Do you work tomorrow?
DJE Nha nómi é Djefri.
​ My name is Jeoffry.
DJI Se nómi é Djika.
​ Her name is Djika.
DJO Pamodi ki bu sta djobe-m?
​ Why are your looking at me?
DJU Kel trabadju la é duru.
​ That job is hard.

PHRASES USING DIGRAPH - LH

LHA Se filha é Médika.
​ Her daughter is a doctor.
LHE Bilheti staba karu.
​ The ticket was expensive.
LHI Nha filhinha sta na jardin.
​ My little girl is in kindergarten.
LHO Un milhon é txeu.
​ One million is a lot.
LHU Na Julhu nu ten Kongresu.
​ In July we will have convention.
